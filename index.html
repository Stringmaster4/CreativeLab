What is JAMM?

Jamm is the best place to collaborate with other people in your hobbies!
There are so many talented people around you that have the same passions as you. We wanted to create a place for people to come to learn and create together, whether it be across oceans or in person.
Come and check out the fantastic talent that have shared their work here already and feel free to share yours!
